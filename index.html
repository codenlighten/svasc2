Willie Nelson

My name is Willie Nelson and I really wanna find my family. I am a 1-2 year old Hound mix that came to SVASC when my mom developed severe allergies to me and was unable to give me the attention that she felt I deserved. I am a very sweet and loving pup! My previous family states that I am a very affectionate dog that enjoys being around my family. I walk okay on leash (but could use some practice), am crate-trained, and am house-trained. Basically, that means we can skip the hard stuff and dive straight into the good stuff... y'know snuggles and walkies and car rides! Oh boy! I get along well with other dogs and wouldn't mind a furry sibling or two. I also do well with kids.
